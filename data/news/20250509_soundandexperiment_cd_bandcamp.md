A series of works by students of the SOUND AND EXPERIMENT seminar (taught by Prof. Florian Hecker) at the University of Music and Performing Arts Munich, in collaboration with Museum Brandhorst as part of the "Fünf Freunde" exhbition has been released on CD and is available for preorder on [Bandcamp](https://soundandexperiment.bandcamp.com/album/sound-and-experiment-x-museum-brandhorst) (releases May 14, 2026).
